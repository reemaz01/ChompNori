# 🥗 Chomp Nori

**Your AI-Powered Nutrition & Meal Planning Assistant**

Chomp Nori is an AI-powered web application that helps users build healthier eating habits by making meal logging, nutrition tracking, and meal planning simple and intelligent. Whether you're trying to eat healthier, maintain a balanced diet, or prepare nutritious meals, Chomp Nori provides personalized insights and actionable recommendations powered by AI.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🍽️ Smart Meal Logging
- Log meals using natural language.
- Quickly record breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
- Keep track of your daily food intake with ease.

### 🤖 AI Nutrition Analysis
Receive an instant nutritional breakdown for every meal, including:
- Calories
- Protein
- Carbohydrates
- Healthy Fats
- Fiber
- Sugar
- Estimated Vitamins & Minerals

### ⚖️ Balanced Diet Recommendations
The AI evaluates your meals and provides suggestions to:
- Improve nutrient balance
- Reduce excessive sugar or sodium
- Increase protein or fiber intake
- Build healthier eating habits

### 🍱 Personalized Meal Prep Ideas
Generate healthy meal suggestions based on:
- Your nutritional needs
- Dietary preferences
- Balanced macronutrients
- Everyday ingredients

### 📊 Daily Nutrition Insights
Monitor your eating habits through:
- Daily meal summaries
- Estimated calorie intake
- Macronutrient distribution
- Overall nutrition quality

## 💡 How It Works

1. Open the Chomp Nori application.
2. Enter or describe your meal.
3. The AI analyzes your meal.
4. Review your nutritional breakdown.
5. Get personalized dietary recommendations.
6. Discover healthy meal prep ideas.
7. Continue tracking meals to build better eating habits.
